My research spans applied mathematics, data science education, and machine learning. My doctoral dissertation focused on probabilistic numerical integration with applications in machine learning. I am currently collaborating with the <a href="https://datascience.uchicago.edu/research/internet-innovation/" target="_blank">Internet Innovation</a> initiative at the UChicago Data Science Institute.
